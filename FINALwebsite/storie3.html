How does that affect us ?





In this website we are going to dive deep into Surveillance Capitalism. First, a definition.

Surveillance Capitalism is a new form of capitalism, exploiting humans without their consent. What Marx dedicated his time to destroy is now way bigger. That's a hope he can't see it, he would either kill himself or emigrate to a remote place to meditate. Actually that may be the reason why some people emigrate to St Pierre et Miquelon to live with birds.

Because people are tired of having their datas taken away and used to make profit. Why ? There are many reasons. First, you loose your liberty. Second, your life quality becomes worse, because screens are improved and steal more and more of your time. Third, you contribute to your own loss, which we will talk about in a few pages.



By the way an author, Shoshana Zuboff, talked about this in a book entitled 'The Age of Surveillance Capitalism', which is the main inspiration of this website.
